About Us

Company Background
South Sudan Youth Vision is a charitable organization which operates in South Sudan with the aim of scaling the Sudanese market by supporting youths with business ideas and those with low capital with entrepreneurial skills, leadership skills, financial literacy  and financial funding. We offered a mentorship program for the selected candidates in each track with a certificate after the program.

Vision
To produce well equipped young entrepreneurs who will change South Sudan.

Mission
To be the leader in capitalizing on and providing access to entrepreneurial opportunities, skills, and funding to young South Sudanese.

Goal
To produce more than 3 million creative and well trained youth that will sharpen the economic aspects of the nation by 2040.

2023
We launched our company in 2023 where we first reached of about 100 youths from different backgrounds in the country for mentorship and support.

South Sudan Youth-vision application eligibility.

1. Must be a South Sudanese or IDP with evidence.

2. Must be within 18 - 35 years

3. Must have an idea or already have business with low capital

4. Applicant must either know English or Arabic

5. The person must be from High School and above and must be from anywhere in the country.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the South Sudan youth-vision program only for South Sudanese? 

Ans. Yes, it’s only for South Sudanese youths.

Does the program need any fees for the program?

Ans. No, there’s no fees requirements for you to be considered for the program, you are only needed to be committed.

Is the program entirely online? What do I need to be fully immersed in the program?

Ans. It's a self paced learning module with weekly mentorship with mentors. You are only needed to engage in the course and complete all the assignments and quizzes assigned for you to be considered for the program and certification.

Will you be given a certificate after the completion of the program?

Ans. Yes, you will get a certificate and you will go to a pitching competition with your peers.

Do I need to complete the application at once?

Ans. No, you don’t need to complete it at once, you can do it as you wish as long as you meet the deadline because those who don't meet the deadline won’t be accepted.

How and when will I know that I am accepted?

Our team will review your application, and emails will be sent out to all applicants stating whether you are selected or not.
